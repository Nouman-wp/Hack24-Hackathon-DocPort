Design Projects

Design involves creating visual and functional solutions across various mediums. It spans several domains, each focusing on different aspects of aesthetics, usability, and user experience.

Key Domains in Design

  • Graphic Design

    Graphic design involves creating visual content for print and digital media. It includes branding, advertising, layout design, and visual communication.

  • Web Design

    Web design focuses on designing and creating websites. It includes user interface (UI) design, user experience (UX) design, and responsive design.

  • Product Design

    Product design involves creating physical products that are functional, aesthetic, and user-friendly. It covers industrial design, prototyping, and usability testing.

  • Interior Design

    Interior design focuses on enhancing the interior of spaces to create a more aesthetically pleasing and functional environment. It includes space planning, furniture design, and color schemes.

  • UX/UI Design

    UX/UI design centers on creating intuitive and user-friendly digital interfaces. UX design focuses on the overall user experience, while UI design deals with the visual layout and interactivity.
